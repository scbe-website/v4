Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How do I know if I need a panel upgrade? +
Signs include frequently tripping breakers, flickering lights, a 100A or smaller panel, or plans to add an EV charger or major appliances. We assess your current panel and give you a straight answer — no upselling.
Do you pull permits, and why does that matter? +
Yes, always. Permitted work is inspected for code compliance — protecting your safety, insurability, and resale value. Unpermitted electrical work can void homeowner's insurance. We handle all permits and inspections.
What does an EV charger installation involve? +
We assess panel capacity, run a dedicated 240V circuit, install the Level 2 charger, pull the permit, and coordinate utility approval. Most installs are done in a single visit. We work with all major brands.
Do you work on biotech and laboratory electrical systems? +
Yes — it's one of our specialties. 12 years of experience with biotech labs, medical buildings, and research facilities. We handle dedicated instrument circuits, isolated power panels, emergency systems, and cleanroom wiring.
Can you add a 30A or 50A RV hookup to my property? +
Yes. We install dedicated 30A (NEMA TT-30) and 50A (NEMA 14-50) RV hookups at residential and commercial properties throughout Southern California, permitted to NEC code.
What areas do you serve? +
We're based in San Diego and serve all of San Diego County (East & North County), Orange County, Los Angeles, San Bernardino, and Riverside. For large commercial projects we accommodate throughout SoCal.
Are you the same as other "Bradley Electric" companies? +
No. SoCal Bradley Electric is an independent, locally owned electrical company based in San Diego. No affiliation with Bradley Electric companies in other states. CA License #1131979 — verify at cslb.ca.gov.
